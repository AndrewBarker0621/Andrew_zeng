Ruihao Zeng

Hi, I am Ruihao Zeng, a Year Four (senior) student undergrad from Fujian Normal University in China.

The main direction of my research is Applied Intelligence and Intelligent Traffic System. I hope to use intelligent methods to solve problems in transportation and society.

I have worked as a research assistant at the Digital Fujian Institute of Transportation Big Data of Fujian University of Technology since October 2020, co-advised by Xing Wang and Fumin Zou .

I worked for a summer research program at Massachusetts Institute of Technology in 2021, supervised by Mark Vogelsberger .

Earlier, I have also worked for a summer program related to data mining in urban city at the Institute of Computing Technology of Chinese Academy of Sciences with the award of top 5% , recommended by Xingwu Liu .

I am looking for an AI & ITS Master/Phd position starting in the 2022 fall.
